Hygeine of head and hair

In this article today we will learn about how to maintain hygenic hair and a hygenic mind as there is not to much to discuss in head.

Hair

Hair matters a lot to the look of a person and controls others viewpoint of him or her. If your hair looks nice you look good and the other way round. If you do not mantain proper hygeine of your hair you will not look good and suffer from many diseases like dandruff. Here are some ways you can maintain proper hygeine:

Wash your hair twice a week with cruelty free and chemical free shampoo.

Do not put water on your hair everyday.

Do not use very hot water to wash your hair.

Mental Health

Mental hygiene or mybriangiene should be mantained because when our mind is clean our work is also clean. We start feeling very stressed and we are not able to do any work. Here are some smart tips to maintain hygeine:

Meditate for 2 - 3 minutes when you start your day.

Clear out old and unwanted memories from your brain everyday.

Refresh your brain every now and then.
